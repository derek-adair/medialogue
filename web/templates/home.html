Medialogue Demo

Medialogue requires a template "base.html". View this here.

Medialogue requries 3 blocks in your base template:

  1. block "extra_head" - anything you want included in the head of your document, typically css, etc.
  2. block "body" - main body of your html pages
  3. block "extra_scripts" - js scripts to be included at the bottom of your document
